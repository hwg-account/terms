LETTER OF APPOINTMENT

I ("the Client") hereby irrevocably and unequivocally agree to engage your Company to provide general guidance and educational support in relation to legacy planning. The Company's role is to assist the Client in understanding available legacy planning structure, including trusts and its related products, and to refer to the Client to licensed and authorized trust companies for further consultation and formal engagement subject to and in accordance with the terms and conditions hereinafter set out.

1. Scope of Services

1.1 The scope of services to be provided by you among others include the followings: -

(a) To assign One (1) Resource Person which duly registered and authorized by the Company to engage with the Client to understand the Client's general financial situation, risk tolerance, objective and other relevant factors;

(b) To share general information or options relating to legacy planning structure, including but not limited to trust and its related products to the Client based on the Client's expressed objectives;

(c) To facilitate the Client's connection to licensed trust companies by providing appropriate referrals and introductions;

(d) To assist in coordinating initial discussions or enquiries between the Client and the referred trust company.

(all of the above services shall be collectively referred to as "the Services")

2. Acknowledgement by the Client

2.1 In consideration of the Company agrees to provide the above Services, the Client hereby irrevocably and unequivocally agrees and acknowledges: -

(a) that the Company's role is limited to sharing general information and referring the Client to a relevant licensed trust company;

(b) that the importance of exercising the Client's own due diligence in evaluating the recommendation provided by the Resource Person and the decision to set up any trust and its related product is solely at the Client's discretion and risk, based on further consultation with the licensed trust company;

(c) that the Client did not rely on the representations made by the Resource Person in making the decision to set up any trust and its related products which all such representations made the Resource Person were merely the Resource Person's recommendation merely serving as a reference and to be construed as a mere puff;

(c) that the Client hereby irrevocably and unequivocally consent to the Company and the Resource Person the right to utilize the personal information or data provided and/or to make available the said personal data as may be necessary to any party including its subsidiary and associate companies in facilitating the efficient and lawful administration of the trust and its related products in compliance with the provisions of the Personal Data Protection Act 2010;

(d) that the trust and its related products recommended and/or shared by the Resource Person which the Client is agreeing to set up are provided and to be provided by the relevant trust companies having the valid licenses issued by the relevant authorities and such products are not provided by the Company which the Company and/ or the Resource Person shall not be held liable nor responsible in whatsoever and howsoever manner should the Client is not satisfy or intends to raise a dispute over such products; and

(d) that the Client shall on a full and frank disclosure provide the true and accurate personal information and details to the Resource Person.

This Letter of Appointment shall be governed by and construed with the laws of Malaysia and the parties hereto agree to submit, subject to recourse to arbitration as herein provided, to the exclusive jurisdiction of the courts of Malaysia.
